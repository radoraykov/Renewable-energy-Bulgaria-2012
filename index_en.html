These charts show all public data for the renewable energy bought by energy distributors in Bulgaria. The original data can be found here and the in cleaned orderly format here. Thanks to Svetla Vasileva for pointing me to this data.
The goal of this visualization is to show us in a more comprehensive way the distribution and types of RES energy production, as well as who is buying it. I've asked for data on convensional power plant production in 2012, so that we compare the rates. If you have access to such data, please contact me at yurukov@gmail.com
You can also visit my other open data projects.

Boyan Yurukov
The data can be filtered from all charts. When you select a filter, the address changes in the address bar. Thus if you open it again or share it, the page will be opened in the same state as you left it. Here are some filter examples:
  • All solar plants built in 2012
  • RES plants built after '89, from which NEC buys power
  • Energy produced in the Batak dam in 2012
  • RES plants, from which EON buys power
  • Wind plants built between 2004-09, from which EVN buys power
  • All Biomass and waste water plants
  • Energy bought by Energo-Pro Sales
  • Hydro with less than 0.5 MW capacity, which sold power from Jan to April 2012
  • Wind farms with more than 5 MW capacity
